About Bloomhaus

Welcome to Bloomhaus Sewing Academy — a creative space where beginners and aspiring makers come to discover the art of sewing. We believe that sewing is a skill anyone can learn, and we're here to make that journey enjoyable, structured, and empowering.

Our 6-week programme is designed to take you from complete beginner to confident maker. Whether you want to create your own clothes, upcycle, or simply discover a new hobby, Bloomhaus is the place to bloom.
